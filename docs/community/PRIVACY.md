# SkillPM Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 27, 2026

SkillPM ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our CLI tool, registry website, and associated services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Analytics and Telemetry
* **Skill Downloads**: We record when a skill is downloaded to track popularity. This includes an anonymized hash of your IP address.
* **Execution Status**: If opted-in, the CLI may send anonymized success/failure data for skill executions to help authors improve their skills.

### 1.2 Account Information
* **GitHub Integration**: If you sign in via GitHub, we collect your GitHub username and email address to manage your contributions.

## 2. How We Use Information
* To provide and maintain our services.
* To generate aggregate analytics for the community.
* To ensure the security and integrity of the skill registry.

## 3. Data Retention
* We retain anonymized analytics indefinitely.
* Account information is retained as long as your account is active.

## 4. Your Rights
* **Access and Portability**: You can request a copy of the data we have associated with your account.
* **Deletion**: You can request the deletion of your account and associated skills.

## 5. Security
We use industry-standard security measures, including sandboxing and manifest signing, to protect your data and the community.
